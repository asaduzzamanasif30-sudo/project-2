What We Do

Skill-Building, Not Politics

Soccer Skills Academy is a small-group soccer training program focused on technical
development for youth players. This is not a travel team. Not a league. Not a high-pressure
elite academy. This is where players get more touches, more reps, and more confidence.

Our Programs

8-Week Skill Blocks

Each block focuses on a specific theme. Players enroll in structured cycles that build
progressively — 1session per week, 60–75 minutes, in groups of 6–8 players.

footprint

Ball Mastery & Footwork

Master close control, turns, and quick feet through progressive drills.

pan_tool_alt

Passing & First Touch

Develop clean receiving and accurate distribution under pressure.

target

Shooting & Finishing

Learn proper technique for power, placement, and composure in front of goal.

swords

1v1 Attacking & Defending

Build confidence in individual duels with attacking moves and defensive positioning.

Game IQ & Decision Making

Read the game, make smarter choices, and play with purpose.
